CyberChef AI helps you discover, generate, and cook delicious recipes with the power of artificial intelligence. Plan meals, generate grocery lists, watch cooking videos, and optimize your nutrition—all from your phone.

- AI Recipe Generator
- Personalized Meal Plans
- Grocery List Creation
- Cooking Video Library
- Nutrition Analysis
- Trending & Recommended Feed
- Secure Account & Profile
- Light & Dark Mode
