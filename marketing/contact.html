Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get access to a trial?

Once you submit the form, our team will provision a sandbox environment within one business day. Enterprise customers with custom data connectors may require a brief onboarding call first.

Do you support on-premise deployments?

Yes. CyberSentinel AI is available as a fully managed cloud service, a hybrid deployment, or an air-gapped on-premise installation. Contact sales to discuss your environment requirements.

Is my data used to train your AI models?

Never. Tenant data is fully isolated and never used for cross-tenant model training. Our AI models are trained on curated, privacy-safe threat intelligence datasets.

What compliance certifications do you hold?

CyberSentinel AI maintains SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and FedRAMP Ready status. HIPAA Business Associate Agreements are available for healthcare customers.

Can I migrate from my existing SIEM?

Yes. We provide native connectors for Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, IBM QRadar, and Elastic SIEM, as well as a migration toolkit and dedicated onboarding support for enterprise transitions.
